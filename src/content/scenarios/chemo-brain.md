---
title: Chemo brain
---

I have a blood cancer. I've been on chemo for a few months and, despite being an MD, am finding it harder and harder to remember things, read, or have a conversation. It's called chemo brain. I'm frustrated because I'm becoming more and more reliant on my smart phone for taking notes and keeping track of things at the same time that it's getting harder and harder for me to use.
