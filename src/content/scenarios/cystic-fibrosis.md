---
title: Cystic fibrosis
---

I have Cystic Fibrosis, which causes me to spend two to three hours a day wrapped in respiratory therapy equipment that vibrates my chest and makes me cough. As an extension, it makes my arms and legs shake, so I sometimes prefer to use the keyboard or wait to do tasks that require a steady touch with a mouse. I also prefer my tablet over my laptop because I can take it anywhere more conveniently, and it's easier to clean germs off of.
